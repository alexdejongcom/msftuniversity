About Alex de Jong

Storyteller · Microsoft Certified Trainer · Cloud Advisor

Hi there — my name is Alex de Jong. My job title is "Storyteller". Anywhere around the world, on stages big and small, I tell stories about Microsoft solutions.

My job is the best in the world: I get to talk about the things I love, and people even pay to listen. That is very different from what I experience at home. 😉

Part of my job is teaching. As a Microsoft Certified Trainer I teach official Microsoft courses at official training partners and Microsoft offices.

I also advise organizations on their cloud journeys. As CTO as a Service I can take a seat at the board table and help organizations shift to become cloud-driven.

And I run a YouTube channel where I share experiences and lectures — check it out.
